01 — About

A Bit
About Me

I'm a Jakarta-based, backend-focused software engineer with over 7 years of hands-on experience shipping production systems in fintech, health-tech, and e-commerce.

At GoPay, I build and maintain core backend services for the digital insurance platform, spanning customer acquisition, policy lifecycle, and recurring subscriptions for millions of users. Before that I handled authentication and KYC flows at Ajaib (Indonesia's leading investment app), helped migrate legacy monoliths into proper microservices at Halodoc, and built a WhatsApp broadcasting SaaS and real-time call center apps at 1Engage from the ground up.

Along the way I've managed to cut Redis costs by 80%, shave 30% off API response times, and ship new features with zero critical incidents. I'm equally comfortable building greenfield systems entirely from scratch as I am jumping into complex, legacy codebases to maintain and scale them. I care about writing code that's clean, easy to maintain, and actually solves the problem.

Right now I'm getting into AI development and infrastructure on the side, but my bread and butter is still backend engineering. I'm always open to exploring complex technical challenges and collaborating with global teams, with a particular interest in the tech ecosystems of Dubai and Australia.
